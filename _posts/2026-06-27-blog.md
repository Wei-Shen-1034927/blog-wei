* First day of 2026 ski season 

It has snowed just enough for Mt Hutt to officially open today. Got the kids up around 6:15, had breakfast then headed out around 7:00. 

Not many cars on the road as Hutt is only opening the Norwester. The drive felt fresh yet familiar. I am having a cold so nose is blocked and running. 

* First day as ski instructor 

Had to shadow Arianne for a school group lesson today. I can still remember some lessons from level 1 training, but it was nice to have a refresh. Yet, I am still not so confident on leading a group into a lesson. I should have 1 or 2 more shadowing sessons. 

Wearing the snowsports uniform feels different. Suddently I felt I am been watched by everyone around. 

Snow is sticky with patches of ice. Made some short turns but didn't feel I was doing the right thing. I need a few more days of skiing to get the feeling back. Plus I am having a cold, so body isn't feeling so well neither.
